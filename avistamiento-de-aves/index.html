Avistamiento de Aves

We will start our day very early exploring the Manialtepec Lagoon, where exists more than 186 species of endemic bird of the Oaxaca shore.

While we walk-through the lagoon , we will enjoy a delicious olla coffe, together with the famous egg bread.

We will be paying attention to the directions of our guide in order to observe and learn about CASA one of the species that lives in this heavenly place.

After finishing the walk-through we will have chance to amaze ourselves with a delicious breakfast next to the lagoon.
