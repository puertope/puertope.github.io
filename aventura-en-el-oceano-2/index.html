Aventura En El Oceano

For this tour, we will catch a boat in an exciting route through the Pacific Ocean waters, and you will have the opportunity to enjoy the magical encounters with different sea animals you will feel your heart beat rise while watching sea turtles, rays, and the always playful dolphins jumping around from side to side.
If you are traveling with us during the months of November through march you might have the chance of watching whales!
This will be for sure an unforgettable moment from your visit to Puerto Escondido.


Schedules:

Return: 8:00 PM
